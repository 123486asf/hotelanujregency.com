About Hotel Anuj Regency

Nestled in the serene and scenic beauty of Dharamshala, Hotel Anuj Regency offers a peaceful retreat for travelers looking for comfort, convenience, and breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Located in the heart of Bhagsunag, McLeod Ganj, our hotel is just a short walk from the famous Bhagsu Waterfall, Bhagsunag Temple, and local cafes and markets.

Whether you are a solo traveler, a couple on a romantic getaway, or a family on vacation, our hotel provides the perfect base to explore the rich culture and natural beauty of Himachal Pradesh.
